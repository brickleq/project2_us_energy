QUICK FACTS ABOUT TEXAS


Texas is the largest energy-producing state and the largest energy-consuming state in the nation. The industrial sector, including its refineries and petrochemical plants, accounts for half of the energy consumed in the state. Texas leads the nation in wind-powered generation and produced one-fourth of all the U.S. wind powered electricity in 2017. Texas wind turbines have produced more electricity than both of the state's nuclear power plants since 2014.
