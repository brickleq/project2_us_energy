Project Description

The U.S. Energy Dashboard will display U.S.-related energy data using charts and visualizations for both the entire US and individual states. The main page of the app features a choropleth map of U.S. energy consumption. The app also allows users to select states and be routed to a page for that stateâ€™s energy data. Each state page has more than 3 charts of data about that state's energy consumption and production. We store our data in a sqlite database and will deploy our app using heroku.
