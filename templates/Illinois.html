QUICK FACTS ABOUT ILLINOIS


Illinois is the fifth-largest energy-consuming state in the nation, and its industrial sector, which includes petroleum refining and coal mining, uses the most energy of any end-use sector in the state. Illinois ranked first in the nation in 2018 in net electricity generation from nuclear power, and the state's six nuclear plants accounted for 12% of U.S. nuclear power generation.
